About us
EEIWA - stands for "Employer & Employee Interaction Web App". Which should allow employees to interact with theyre manager easily without even needing to meet him in person, for example if an employee needs a day off he can easily come to this app, then calendar and pick a free day that he needs or several days if needed, after picking the day manager automaticaly gets an notification about his employee asking for a day off all its left to confirm or decline.
